The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American drama film directed by Frank Darabont and based on the novella by Stephen King. It follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a man sentenced to two consecutive life sentences in prison for a crime he didn't commit. The movie explores themes of hope, friendship, and resilience.
